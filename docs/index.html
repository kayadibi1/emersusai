FAQ

Questions people will ask before joining

What makes this different from generic AI chatbots?

The positioning is narrower and more useful: fitness, nutrition, and supplements through a science-based lens.

Is this meant to replace a doctor or registered dietitian?

No. The product should support education and decision-making, not replace medical care or individualized clinical treatment.

Will it only be for advanced lifters?

No. The strongest value is turning confusing topics into clear, useful guidance for beginners and experienced users alike.
