Evidence grading

When we know — and when we don't

Every answer carries a confidence label. We don't pretend to know what the literature can't yet support.

Strong

Multiple meta-analyses agree. Recommendation is safe to act on.

Moderate

Evidence is leaning but not decisive. Weigh against context.

Limited

A few small studies. Personal experimentation may be needed.

Insufficient

Not enough rigorous evidence yet. We say so explicitly.

FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a general-purpose model. Emersus is grounded in over a million peer-reviewed exercise-science and nutrition papers. When you ask a question, we retrieve the actual evidence first, then synthesize. Every claim is traceable.

What kind of training does it cover?

Strength & hypertrophy, endurance (running, cycling, rowing), swimming, and climbing. Plus nutrition, supplements, recovery, sleep, and metabolic health.

Is this medical advice?

No. Emersus is an evidence-aware fitness coaching tool. Always consult a clinician for medical decisions, especially around injuries, medications, or chronic conditions.

Can I see the actual studies?

Yes — every cited source links to PubMed or DOI. You can read the abstract, check the methodology, and verify the claim yourself.

How do you handle data privacy?

All training and nutrition data is encrypted, scoped to your account, and never sold. See our Privacy Policy and Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy for full details.

When can I sign up?

Emersus is in private beta. Request access above and we'll email you when a spot opens.
